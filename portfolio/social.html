AI-Powered Social Media Manager

This project is an AI-driven social media management platform that automates content creation, scheduling, and engagement across multiple platforms. Built with a scalable backend and a modern frontend, the application allows users to provide their niche and account credentials, after which the system manages their pages automatically using AI-powered strategies. Key features include:

  • Automated Content Creation: Uses AI models to generate posts, captions, and hashtags tailored to the user’s niche.
  • Multi-Platform Integration: Connects to major social platforms (Instagram, Facebook, Twitter/X, LinkedIn) via APIs for seamless publishing.
  • Smart Scheduling: Optimizes post timing based on engagement data and platform analytics to maximize reach.
  • Audience Insights: Provides dashboards with AI-driven analytics on engagement, follower growth, and content performance.
  • Credential-Based Access: Users securely connect their accounts, and the platform handles authentication and page management.
  • AI-Driven Strategy: Suggests trending topics, competitor analysis, and personalized recommendations to grow page visibility.

The platform simplifies social media growth by combining AI-driven content generation with automated management tools. Users only need to specify their niche and connect their accounts, and the system takes care of content planning, publishing, and analytics—helping individuals and businesses scale their online presence effortlessly.
