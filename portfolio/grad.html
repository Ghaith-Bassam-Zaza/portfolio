Digital Twin for MTGR DB4 (lt-s4.db) Car

This project focuses on creating a digital twin for the MTGR DB4 (lt-s4.db) car integrated with AI for self-driving capabilities. The key features of this project include:

  • Digital Twin: Creates a comprehensive digital replica of the MTGR DB4 car in addition to the campus where the digital twin was tested.
  • AI Integration: Utilizes advanced AI algorithms to enable self-driving capabilities using full self-driving stack (planning, localiztion, perception and control).
  • Real-time Data: Integrates real-time data from multiple sensors from accurate simulation to move the vehicle in real life.
  • Machine Learning: Employs computer vision models for self-driving car perception.
  • User Interface: Provides an interactive user interface for monitoring and controlling the digital twin and cusomizing physical properties.

The project aims to enhance the understanding and development of self-driving technologies, providing valuable insights and a testing ground for future advancements.
