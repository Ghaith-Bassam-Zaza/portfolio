Fast Food Delivery App Project

This project encompasses the full software design for a Fast Food Delivery app, including detailed UML diagrams and design views created using Adobe XD. The app caters to the needs of fast food businesses and customers by providing a seamless ordering and delivery experience.

With an emphasis on user-centric design, the app features intuitive interfaces allowing users to easily browse menus, place orders, and track deliveries in real time. The backend supports robust order management and integrates with payment gateways to ensure smooth transactions.

The software design includes comprehensive UML diagrams that map out system architecture, including user interactions, database schemas, and process flows. Adobe XD designs offer a visual representation of the user experience from onboarding to order completion.

Key features of the app include real-time order tracking, secure payment processing, and customizable restaurant profiles. The app aims to enhance efficiency and customer satisfaction in the fast food delivery industry.
